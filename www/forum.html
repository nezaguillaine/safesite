Why Safe Site application?

  • Generation gap bridging

  • The main aim of Safe Site is to bring together senior and junior civil engineers (and other professional in that field) to build a synergy to work together for sustainable infrastructure development of Rwanda and the region at large.

    Sustainable development is not possible if everyone is trying to grow at his side ignoring the remaining group of people they have same interest. Thus comes the necessity to bridging the gap between generations through use of technologies. As people are now familiar with mobile and social media application, it appeared to us that the right solution to the problem we are trying to solve will require the use of the existing tools and technology and this is the reason why we are bringing to civil engineers a tool which is their to use, communication, interact and build relationships for better collaboration in the future.

  • Knowledge sharing
  • Mentee - Mentor Relationship

    Senior engineer who are willing to share there experience with junior engineers through mentee - mentor relationship as mentors. This will help junior engineers who are lacking place to interact with experience people to have someone dedicated to their carrier development and with whom they can ask any question, related to their field of interest and challenges they have to be ready to face to grow.
    One may argue what will senior engineers gain by mentoring a junior engineers? One day a distinguished professor said that What you teach you master it. Which means that by mentoring someone seniors engineers will not be wasting time but it will be an opportunity they are given to master what they know and to be enriched by questions they will be asked by those junior engineers they will be nurturing.

Who can use it?

  • Accredited engineers

  • To interact among themselves to mentor junior engineers.

  • Junior engineers

  • As a discussion and brainstorming platform offered to them

  • Infrastructure development professionals

  • As a knowledge repository they can consult for different matters.

How to use it?

  • Requirements

  • Internet connection is required to access the platform

  • Login

  • Identification using email and password is required to access platform
